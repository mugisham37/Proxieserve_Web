Payments — edge cases.

Money is where trust is won or lost. Every failure path here is designed to never leave the user wondering whether they were charged — and to make recovery one tap away.

01 / Mobile money — prompt timed out

"The prompt expired"

Timeout

The prompt expired.

We didn't get an approval within 2 minutes. No money has left your account. Want to try again?

Use card instead
No ambiguity. We always state "no money has left your account" when a charge never completed. The server treats the transaction as EXPIRED and any later approval of the stale prompt is rejected with a refund-safe idempotency guard.
02 / Declined — insufficient funds

"Payment didn't go through"

Declined

Payment declined.

Your provider declined the charge — most often that's insufficient balance. Nothing was deducted. Top up and try again, or use another method.

Choose another method
Why declined? We show the provider's plain-language reason where given (insufficient funds, limit reached, blocked card). We never show raw gateway codes.
Decline reasons mapped. Gateway codes are translated to one of six human reasons. The retry preserves everything. After 3 declines we suggest contacting the bank or switching methods.

06 / Dispute or request a refund

"Something wrong with this charge?"

Dispute

Report a problem

Tell us what's wrong with the RWF 15,000 charge on 23 May. We respond within one working day.

Refund policy in plain words. Service fee fully refundable if the failure was ours; government fees follow each office's rules. The dispute creates a ticket in admin oversight (F9) with the full transaction trail attached.
07 / Refund issued

"Your refund is on its way"

Refunded

Refund issued.

We've refunded RWF 15,000 to your MTN MoMo. Mobile money refunds usually arrive within 2 hours; cards take 5–10 business days.

View refund receipt
Channel-aware timing. We set expectations by method — MoMo is near-instant, cards are slow. The refund generates its own receipt object (negative amount) linked to the original transaction.
08 / Connection lost mid-payment

"We're checking what happened"

Network

Checking your payment.

Your connection dropped while we were confirming. Don't pay again yet — we're verifying with your provider. This usually resolves in under a minute.

RECONCILING · TXN MP260523.1432

Reconciliation, not re-charge. If the client returns mid-flight, we poll the provider with the existing transaction reference rather than starting a new charge. The "don't pay again" message prevents panic double-payment — the single most damaging payment bug.
09 / 3-D Secure failed / abandoned

"Bank verification didn't complete"

3DS fail

Verification incomplete.

Your bank couldn't confirm the payment — the code may have been wrong or the window closed. No charge was made. Try again or use Mobile Money.

Use Mobile Money
3DS is the bank's domain. We can't see why it failed (by design). We offer retry + an alternative method, and reassure no charge was made — the authorisation only completes after successful 3DS.

Never charge a stale amount. The fee is re-validated server-side at the moment of charge. If it changed, we block the charge, show the diff, and require fresh confirmation — the client always knowingly approves the exact amount.
