Key design decisions

Three calls worth flagging.

The tracker is the most-revisited page in ProxiServe — most applicants check daily. These three calls keep the experience calm even when the news isn't.

01 / Status drives color

One enum, five hero variants

Magenta for in-progress, info-blue for received, ok-green for completed, warn-yellow for action required and on-hold, danger-red for rejected. Color is functional, not decorative — a user can identify their status by glance, even before reading.

02 / Always actionable

Hero + next step

Action required and Rejected states never just describe — they always show the next button (Upload, Talk to agent, Request refund). Completed shows an outcome card with collection options. Even On-hold gets a "talk to your agent" affordance.

03 / Off-platform parity

SMS + WhatsApp = same info

Every status change fires a templated SMS (always) and WhatsApp template (if opted in), both linking to the public tracker URL. Half our users never visit the web tracker — they live in WhatsApp. The status is everywhere it needs to be.
