06 / Common questions

You probably want to know.

Short answers below. For everything else, our team is on WhatsApp.

Is my data safe with ProxiServe? +
Yes. Every document you send is encrypted in transit and at rest. Only the agent assigned to your application can see your details, and we keep an audit log of every access. We never sell or share your information. Read our privacy policy.
Do I need an account to apply? +
No. You can apply as a guest and use your tracking code to check progress. If you want to manage multiple applications in one place, you can create an account later — your first application links to it automatically.
How long does it take? +
It depends on the government office, but most services finish in 2–10 working days. Each service page shows the typical turnaround. We'll let you know if anything slows down.
How do I pay? +
MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, or card. You can also pay your assigned agent directly. You only pay once we agree on the fee — never before.
What if my application is rejected? +
We'll explain why in plain language and tell you the options — appeal, re-apply with corrections, or get a refund of our service fee if the rejection was our error. Government fees are non-refundable.
Can I cancel? +
Yes, anytime before we submit to the authority. Once submitted we can usually withdraw too, but some government fees may not be refundable.
Who are the agents? +
They're trained professionals who've handled hundreds of applications each. They're ID-verified, bound by confidentiality, and reachable directly from your tracking page.
