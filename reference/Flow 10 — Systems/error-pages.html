FLOW 10 / ERROR & SYSTEM PAGES · 6 PAGES

When things go wrong.

Errors are part of the product. Each one stays on-brand, explains what happened in plain language, never blames the user, and always offers a way forward — including offline, where the app still does what it can.

01 / 404 — page not found

Lost, not stuck

404

We can't find that page.

The link might be old, or there's a typo. Let's get you back somewhere useful.

Go home →Track an application
Contextual escape hatches. The 404 knows roughly where you came from (service URL, dashboard, tracker) and offers the most relevant next step. Broken slugs are logged so admin can add redirects for common typos.
02 / 500 — something broke

Our fault, not yours

500

Something went wrong on our end.

Your data is safe — nothing was lost. We've logged it for our team. Try again in a moment.

Try again ↻Contact support

Incident #INC-26-3819

Honest + traceable. Always takes the blame off the user, reassures about data, and surfaces an incident ID that routes to the on-call engineer. Auto-logged to Sentry with full request context.
03 / Maintenance mode

Back shortly

We're doing a little maintenance.

ProxiServe is briefly offline for an upgrade. We'll be back by 14:00 CAT. Your applications are safe and waiting.

Check status page
Triggered from F9. The admin maintenance switch shows this page to the public + client zones while staff work continues. Includes an ETA and a link to the status page. Returns a 503 with Retry-After for crawlers.
04 / Offline — no connection

Still useful

You're offline.

No internet right now. You can still view applications you've already opened — we saved them on this device. New actions will sync when you're back.

View saved applicationsRetry ↻
The PWA shell speaks. Served by the service worker when the network's down. Cached pages remain viewable; writes queue. Auto-dismisses with a toast the moment connection returns.
05 / Session expired

Sign in again

⏱

You've been signed out.

For your security we sign you out after a while. Your unsaved work is preserved — sign back in to pick up exactly where you left off.

Sign in →
Preserves work. Rather than bouncing to a cold login, this preserves the in-memory state (draft message, half-filled form) and restores it after re-auth — matching the F6 re-auth modal pattern.
06 / 429 — too many requests

Slow down

429

That's a lot of requests.

You're going faster than we allow, to keep things fair for everyone. Take a short breather and try again in a few seconds.

Try again in 5s
Gentle throttle. Protects the API from abuse and runaway scripts. The retry button counts down to when the window resets (from the Retry-After header). Never punishes — just paces.

One error system, every zone

All six pages share the same structure: a large serif numeral or icon, a plain-language headline that never blames the user, a one-sentence explanation, and at least one clear way forward. They inherit the cream-and-ink palette so even a 500 feels like part of ProxiServe. Staff zones (F8/F9) render the same errors in their dark theme. Every error is keyboard-navigable and screen-reader announced via role="alert".
