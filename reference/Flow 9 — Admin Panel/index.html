Key design decisions

Three calls worth flagging.

Admin is the zone with the most reach into the rest of the system, so its choices are about correctness and accountability over polish.

01 / Schema is the contract

One builder, three surfaces

The schema you build here renders the F3 wizard step 2 and the F2 service page through the same renderer. Versioning + a migration dialog on publish mean editing a live form never breaks the 142 people halfway through it.

02 / Tamper-evident by design

The audit log can't be edited

Append-only, hash-chained, no edit/delete affordance anywhere — not even for the owner. A government-adjacent product handling ID documents must be able to prove who did what, for seven years.

03 / Power with friction

Consequence sets the friction

Archive is one click; permanent delete needs you to type the name. Viewing an agent's case is logged; intervening needs a reason and notifies them. The more damage an action can do, the more deliberate it has to be.
