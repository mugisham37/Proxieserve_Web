02 / How this service works

Five days, three touchpoints.

Most of the wait is the Immigration office — we keep you posted the whole way through.

01

You send us your details

You finish the 5-step application from your phone or computer — usually under 10 minutes. Your documents are encrypted the moment they upload.

02

We prepare & submit

Within 1 working day, an agent reviews everything, fills the official Immigration form, and submits it. You'll get a tracking code by SMS.

03

You receive your passport

Immigration prints the new passport and returns it to us in 4–6 days. We hand it to you in person at our office or by courier.

04 / Questions about this service

Asked often.

Specific to passport renewal. For everything else, see the general FAQ.

What if my passport is already expired? +
No problem — this service is specifically for renewals, and an expired passport is fine. We use it to verify identity. If it was stolen or lost, you'll need a police clearance certificate too (we can help you get one).
Can I renew if I'm abroad? +
Only if you can come to Rwanda for the biometric appointment — Immigration requires it in person. We can hold your application until you arrive and book the appointment for the day you land.
How long is the new passport valid for? +
10 years for adult applicants (18+), 5 years for minors. The expiry date will be printed on your new bio page.
What if my name has changed? +
You'll need a marriage certificate, deed poll, or court order documenting the change. Upload it during step 2 and your agent will confirm whether Immigration accepts your specific documentation before proceeding.
Can I get an urgent passport same-day? +
Immigration's fastest official turnaround is 2 working days, and that's what our Urgent tier targets. Same-day is not currently possible through any channel.
